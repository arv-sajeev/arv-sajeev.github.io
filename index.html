ABOUT ME

Hi,

I'm Arvind, I prefer to call myself "The Raving Sage" on the web but that hasn't actually caught on with other people just yet. The moniker is sort of a twisted anagram of my name and it's something that I put a lot of thought into. I completed my undergrad in CSE from College of Engineering Trivandrum, graduated in 2019. My interests are scattered all over the place but most of them lie close to the hardware, operating systems, computer networks and computer architecture just to name a few. I currently work as a Systems Engineer in TCS. I tend to write code once in a while, most of it is updated on Github. I'm not what you'd call a "Gifted programmer", but I always try to challenge myself and get better. Over the years I've sort of fallen in love with the art that I devote lots of my free time learning stuff or writing code. I'm familiar with C, C++, python, bash, assembly, javascript and java in exactly that order. Like someone once said "Where words stop music begins", I've always been fascinated by music and it's power to elicit emotions and unite people.It's a deeply personal and collaborative experience at the same time. I tend to restrict my singing to the bathroom or in concerts where it is too loud for anyone to hear me but luckily I'm quite comfortable with instruments like the keyboard, guitar and most recently the murali and they allow me to express myself musically to a great extent. These skills that I acquired over many years I completely owe and dedicate to numerous loved ones, my parents, brother, instructors and many others.
